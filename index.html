The Problem

Traditional compliance is broken

Food & beverage manufacturers face a compliance system that was never designed for the complexity of modern operations.

Manual evidence collection

QA teams spend 60–70% of their time gathering data, chasing signatures, and preparing documentation — not actually improving quality.

Compliance costs the food industry $75B per year. In 2024 alone, recalls totaled $1.92B. The current model is unsustainable.
